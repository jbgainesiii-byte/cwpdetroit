What we do, and what we never do.

Cleaning With Purpose is a home cleaning and family communication service. It is not home health care, and you should hear that from us, plainly.

We always

  • Clean the home with care and consistency
  • Send your family a warm note after every visit
  • Mention practical things we notice, like supplies running low
  • Treat your parent with the dignity of a neighbor, not a patient

We never

  • Provide medical care or health advice
  • Handle or remind about medication
  • Help with bathing, dressing, or mobility
  • Monitor health or wellness
  • Act as an emergency response service

If your family needs those services, we’ll gladly point you toward licensed home care providers, and we work alongside them well. And if we ever arrive and something is clearly wrong, we call 911 first and you second — that’s not a service, that’s what a decent person does.

Questions families ask

Asked and answered, honestly.

Is this medical or personal care?
No, and we say that proudly. We’re a cleaning service that keeps families in the loop. For personal or medical care, you’ll want a licensed home care agency, and we’re happy to work alongside one.
Will my dad feel watched?
That’s the thing we protect hardest. To your father, this is a cleaning service, full stop. The note comes to you quietly, it’s warm instead of clinical, and nothing is ever photographed without the permission your family sets up front.
What if my mom says she doesn’t need help?
Most of them say that. That’s why the first step is a walkthrough, not a commitment. She meets one person, not a company. In our experience the resistance isn’t to help. It’s to strangers and to fuss, and we bring neither.
How do you handle keys, codes, and cameras?
With a written access policy set before we arrive: keys, lockboxes, codes, parking, pets, all of it. Cameras in the home are completely fine with us.
Why does the first visit cost more?
The Purpose Home Reset is deeper work: it brings the home back to a comfortable baseline and teaches us its priorities. That’s what lets us quote your recurring plan honestly. It’s a reset, not a discounted trial.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Metro Detroit and the surrounding communities: Detroit, Dearborn, Royal Oak, Ferndale, Southfield, Troy, Warren, Grosse Pointe, and Livonia. Not sure we reach the neighborhood? Just ask, and you’ll get a straight answer the same day.
